Best Ways to Clean Your Sofas

Pre-Inspection

An expert cleaning technician will inspect the sofa before the actual cleaning process begins. Fabric identification is the first thing to determine the type of the fabric. Different fabrics require different cleaning methods and cleaning solutions.

The cleaning technician will assess the condition of the sofa for any existing, tear, stain, rips, or other damages. The technician will deeply analyze if any stain is present, identify the type of stain, and its severity, and determine the most effective stain removal for the particular stain.

The technician will select an appropriate cleaning method based on the fabric of the sofa, condition, and stains. The technician will discuss the findings of the pre-inspection with the customer and will suggest the most effective cleaning method for your sofa.

Deep Cleaning

The deep cleaning process helps your sofa to shine like a new one. If there are stubborn stains, pretreatment with a specialized cleaning solution will take place to loosen and break down the grime before starting the real cleaning process.

There are different cleaning methods depending on the fabric of the sofa including steam cleaning, dry wash, and shampooing. In steam cleaning hot water and steam are used to remove the stains. This is really effective in disinfecting the sofa by killing dust mites and bacteria.

During shampooing, a cleaning solution is applied to the sofa fabric to loosen and remove dirt. This method is ideal for heavily soiled sofas. Dry cleaning is used to remove the dirt from sofas where wet cleaning cannot be performed. A solvent-based solution will be applied to delicate fabrics.

Odor & Stain Removal

Stains, whether from food, drink, ink, or playful pets, come in many forms. Pinpointing the exact culprit is key to effective treatment. While some stains yield easily, others, particularly stubborn ones, require specialized care and targeted methods for successful removal.

After inspecting the type of fabric and the type of stain, a specialized approach for stain removal takes place. There are different types of stain removal like steam cleaning, dry washing, and shampooing.

Odor removal is important to keep the sofa fresh and fragrant. Deodorizing sprays or powders are used to effectively remove unpleasant smells, leaving the sofa fresh and clean. This process will keep the sofa fresh and odor-free for a long period.

Custom Cleaning

Customized cleaning is a specialized service best suitable for large sofas, uniquely shaped sofas, and sofas having special built-in features. These sofas need specialized care and specialized solutions to prevent any damage that may happen to the delicate fabrics.

A detailed pre-inspection takes place where the identification of the fabrics, stain analysis, and a customized cleaning plan will be made. The targeted cleaning process focuses on stubborn stains and heavily soiled areas. Deep cleaning is performed in these areas.

Delicate fabrics like silk and velvet receive specialized care and gentle cleaning techniques. Intricate designs are given meticulous attention, and built-in features like recliners and sleeper beds are expertly cleaned using appropriate methods.
